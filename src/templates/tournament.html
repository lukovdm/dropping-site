Rising high on mushrooms

The tournament is organised by a fucking plumber high on shrooms. the tournament has a weird set up.

The tournament starts with 4 brackets. this will decide which poul you go to. The winners of the bracket will play in the finals, consisting of 4 players. The second place of the bracket will play for the 5-8th spots, and third and fourth place will play in the losers poul (9-15th place).

Teams are placed in brackets according to there seeding. The first four seeds are in the first bracket, the next four in the second bracket and so on. The first and second seeded in a bracket play against each other, the third and fourth do too. When there is no opponent available for a match, the other team automatically wins.

The seeding

In every poul a round-robin is played. After the round-robin the placement is decided by the following rules. If the first rule does not decide a placement for two teams, the next rule is used and so on.

  1. Number of wins in the poul, higher is better
  2. Alphabetical order of 2 letter of their name, lower is better
  3. Points scored in lost games in the poul, higher is better
  4. Date of release by nintendo, earlier is better
